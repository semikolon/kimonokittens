## Legal Analysis

### Which Law Applies?

**Privatuthyrningslagen (2012:978)** likely applies because:

1. **Owner is a private individual** (Stefan Dusing, personnummer) - NOT a company
2. **Property is a villa** - single-family home, not apartment building
3. **Bostadsagenturen is a broker** ("Förmedlare"), not the landlord
4. **No indication of commercial rental operation**

### What Privatuthyrningslagen Says

Under privatuthyrningslagen (lagen om uthyrning av egen bostad):

- **Tenant's notice period**: **1 month** (mandatory minimum, cannot be contracted away)
- **Landlord's notice period**: 3 months (can be longer by agreement)

**Key point**: Even if the contract says 2 months, **the tenant can always give 1 month notice** under privatuthyrningslagen. The contractual term is simply void as it's worse than the legal minimum.

### Relevant Sections

From privatuthyrningslagen (2012:978):

> **3 §** Hyresgästen får säga upp hyresavtalet att upphöra att gälla vid det månadsskifte som inträffar tidigast efter en månad från uppsägningen.

Translation: "The tenant may terminate the lease to cease at the monthly shift occurring at earliest after one month from the notice."

This is a **tenant protection rule** - contractual terms offering less favorable conditions are invalid.
